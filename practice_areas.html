Notable Cases

  • 4.5 million dollar settlement for a brain injured child due to a failure to diagnose bacterial meningitis.

  • 900,000 dollar settlement during trial of the death of a patient for failure to diagnose bladder cancer.
  • 600,000 dollar settlement during trial for negligence of the death of a patient in a nursing home who developed a sacral decubitus ulcer.
